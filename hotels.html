The Sarai Suites, Murree

Bari Imam Shrine is 46 km from The Sarai Suites, Murree, while Pakistan National Council of Arts is 48 km from the property. The nearest airport is Islamabad International Airport, 78 km from the accommodation.

Hotel Reego skardu

Breakfast is available daily, and includes continental, Asian and halal options. Speaking English and Urdu at the reception, staff are willing to help at any time of the day. The nearest airport is Skardu Airport, 10 km from the accommodation. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.3 for a two-person trip.

Jovial Gold Naran

Jovial Gold Naran is offering accommodation in Nārān. With free WiFi, this 4-star hotel has a garden and a terrace. There is a restaurant serving American cuisine, and free private parking is available. Every room comes with a TV, and certain units at the hotel have a mountain view. At Jovial Gold Naran all rooms are fitted with bed linen and towels.
